General Background

Kennedy Wanjiru Maina was born on 5th June 1989 in Kanuku Village, California Ward, Nairobi county. Kennedy is the eldest in a family of four and he is married and blessed with one child. Besides politics, Kennedy is a paralegal officer and social activist well known in Kamukunji. Kennedy works with N.G.Os such as UNICEF His work focuses protecting children from matters such as Gender Based Violence(G.B.V), promoting their education and Early marriages. Moreover, he works with other organisations that empowers the youth on issues affecting them such as drugs and substance abuse, providing incentives and other support required to start a business of their choice.

Education Background

Tertiary Education

Kennedy went toMaseno University from 2009 to 2011 and has a Diploma in Community development. He also has a certificate in psychology from the same institute.

High School education

Kennedy went toMaina Wanjigi High School located in Eastleigh Nairobi County.

Primary School

Kennedy went to Heshima Primary School located in Shaurimoyo estate Nairobi county

My Life Principle and vision

Principle

To uphold integrity and intense of responsibility in all tasks or assignments entrusted to me and to make invaluable contribution towards realization of the goals and objectives of the institution.

Vision

To work with the citizens of Kenya especially those of California ward to improve the living standards of the people through creation of emploment opportunities, fostering education, nurturing talent, promoting self-help groups and improving disaster prevention, response and mitigation
