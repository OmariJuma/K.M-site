General Background

Kennedy Maina Wanjiru was born on 22nd January 1988 in Kanuku Village, California Ward, Nairobi county. Kennedy is the eldest in a family of four and he is married and blessed with one child. Besides politics, Kennedy is a paralegal officer and social activist well known in Kamukunji. Kennedy works with N.G.Os such as UNICEF His work focuses protecting children from matters such as Gender Based Violence(G.B.V), promoting their education and Early marriages. Moreover, he works with other organisations that empowers the youth on issues affecting them such as drugs and substance abuse, providing incentives and other support required to start a business of their choice.
