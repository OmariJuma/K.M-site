Hello good people,my name is Kennedy Maina aspiring MCA for California ward 2022 via the O.D.M political party. I am not vying for this seat for the first time. I've been in the ballot from 2013, 2017 to no luck but this year I want you to make the right choice, why? Because its your constitutional right to elect a leader who will serve and address your grievances. Whether am vying via ODM, Jubilee, U.D.A or independent I will serve everyone irregardless of their tribe, age, social class or whether they voted for me or not.

Its the right time for me to take charge of California because as you have experienced my predecessors have not given you the service and attention that you have been craving for.
