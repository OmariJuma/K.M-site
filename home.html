Kennedy Maina California ward M.C.A 2022

Hello good people, my name is Kennedy Maina, I live in California ward-Biafra Kanuku to be precise. I am vying for California ward M.C.A 2022 via the O.D.M political party. I am not vying for this seat for the first time. I've been in the ballot from 2013, 2017 to no luck but this year I want you to make the right choice, why? Because its your constitutional right to elect a leader who will serve and address your grievances. Whether am vying via ODM, Jubilee, U.D.A or independent I will serve everyone irregardless of their tribe, age, social class or whether they voted for me or not.

Its the right time for me to take charge of California ward because as you have experienced my predecessors have not given you the service and attention that you have been craving for.

My Manifestos

If elected the following is what I want to do for the people of California ward:

“1) To work with the county and national government to promote education through issuance of bursaries and scholarships to performing students”

“2) Foster girl child education and support through providing needs such as sanitary towels and peer counselling sessions”

“3) Foster and nurture talent through competitions and providing professional mentors”

“4) Provide support to business oriented people and self help groups”

“5) Improve disaster mitigation and action plan in the ward through availing ambulances and firebrigade services in the area”

“6) Work with N.M.S to provide employment opportnities to our youth ”

“7) Prevent unlawful arrests and detentions of the youths in the area. ”
